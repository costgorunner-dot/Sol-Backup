## Notes for LTX-2 / ComfyUI

**Speed Modifiers:**
- Slow: subtle, cinematic
- Fast: dynamic, urgent

**Movement Types:**
- **Dolly:** Forward/backward through space
- **Pan:** Horizontal rotation (tripod)
- **Tilt:** Vertical rotation (tripod)
- **Truck:** Lateral movement (side to side)
- **Pedestal:** Vertical movement (up/down)
- **Arc/Circle:** Curved paths around subject
- **Crane:** Large vertical movements
- **Drone:** Aerial perspectives

**Combine with:**
- Static camera LoRA for locked shots
- Subject motion for dynamic scenes
- Multiple movements for complex sequences
