- Original: 5k chunks (too many API calls, fact extraction loses context)
- First optimization: 10k chunks (better, but still slow)
- Considered: 15k chunks (local AI advised against — too small, quality risk)

**Key Insight:** Hindsight needs larger chunks (32k+ context window minimum) because consolidation builds relationships, not just stores facts.

**Why Large Chunks Matter:**

- Too large = fact extraction gets messy, consolidation struggles

**Optimal Range for GLM 4.6 (128k capacity):**
- 10k chunks is likely in good territory

- Practical ceiling may be Hindsight's 32k minimum requirement
