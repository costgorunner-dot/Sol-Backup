### Key Discoveries

**Spatial Upscaler (Game-Changer):**
- Works in latent space (before pixels decoded)
- Adds detail based on semantic understanding
- Face gets face-like detail, fabric gets fabric texture, water gets water ripples
- Best practice: Generate at 480p-720p → Upscale to 4K
- Two 2x passes better than one 4x pass

**RTX Video Upscaler (Fast Alternative):**
- Hardware-accelerated by NVIDIA
- Real-time 4K upscaling (~5 seconds for 5s video)
- 30-60x faster than Spatial Upscaler
- Works on 3070 Ti 8GB (Sol's hardware compatible!)

**Hybrid Workflow Strategy:**
1. Generate at 480 x 864, 33 frames (2 sec at 16fps)
2. Spatial Upscale to 960 x 1728
3. RTX Video to 4K (3840 x 2160)
4. Post-processing (color grading, audio sync)

### Technical Constraints Learned
- Resolution must be divisible by 32
- Frame counts must be 8n + 1 (9, 17, 25, 33, 41, 49, 57...)
- VRAM: Spatial needs 12-16GB, RTX needs 8GB
