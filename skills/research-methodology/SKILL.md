# Research Methodology Skill

**Purpose:** Better research practices for Sol - verified, cross-referenced, multi-source methodology

**When to use:** Before ANY research task (pricing, performance, comparisons, etc.)

---

## 📋 Pre-Research Checklist

**Before starting ANY search, ask:**
1. What am I trying to find out?
2. What type of information do I need? (pricing, performance, reviews, specs)
3. How many sources should I check? (minimum 3-5)
4. What search tools will I use? (Brave, Tavily, multiple)

---

## 🔍 Core Methodologies

### **SIFT Technique**
- **Stop** - Don't trust first result
- **Investigate** - Check source credibility
- **Find** - Consult multiple sources
- **Trace** - Follow claims to original context

### **Lateral Reading**
- **Research the claim, not just the source**
- Cross-check with multiple reputable sources
- Compare and contrast information
- Use fact-checking sites when available

### **P.R.O.V.E.N. Framework**
- **Purpose** - Why was this created?
- **Relevance** - Does it answer the question?
- **Objectivity** - Is there bias?
- **Verifiability** - Can claims be verified?
- **Expertise** - Is the source authoritative?
- **Newness** - Is it current?

---

## ⚠️ Common Pitfalls to AVOID

### **Search Bias:**
- ❌ **Top 5 results ≠ Best results** (often paid/SEO)
- ❌ **First page ≠ Most accurate** (can be gamed)
- ❌ **Single source** (always verify)
- ❌ **Outdated information** (check dates)
- ❌ **Different context** (WAN2.1 ≠ LTX-2)

### **AI-Specific Issues:**
- ❌ Black box bias
- ❌ Lack of diversity in data
- ❌ SEO-driven content over accuracy
- ❌ Taking first answer without verification

## ✅ Better Research Process

### **Step 1: Multiple Search Tools**
- Use Brave Search AND Tavily (when available)
- Check beyond first page of results
- Use different search queries for same topic

### **Step 2: Source Diversity**
- **Official sources:** Manufacturer, .gov, .edu
- **Retailers:** Amazon, Newegg, Best Buy, B&H, eBay
- **User experiences:** Reddit, GitHub, forums, Discord
- **Reviews:** Multiple independent sources
- **Fact-checking sites:** Snopes, PolitiFact, etc.

### **Step 3: Cross-Reference Everything**
- Find **3-5 different sources** for same claim
- **Compare and contrast** - Do they agree?
- **Check official data** - Manufacturer specs, government stats
- **Look for consensus** - What do most sources say?
- **Note discrepancies** - Why do sources disagree?

### **Step 4: Verify Claims**
- **Trace back to original source**
- **Check dates** - Is it current?
- **Verify statistics** - Official data?
- **Look for corrections/updates**
- **Test with real users** when possible

### **Step 5: Be Transparent**
- **Show methodology** - How did you research?
- **Cite multiple sources** - Not just one
- **Acknowledge uncertainty** - When not sure
- **Admit mistakes** - When wrong
- **Show your work** - Explain process

## 📊 Specific Research Types

### **Pricing Research:**
1. Check manufacturer direct price
2. Check 3+ major retailers (Amazon, Newegg, Best Buy, B&H)
3. Check used markets (eBay, Facebook Marketplace)
4. Look for sales/discounts
5. Note regional pricing differences
6. **Minimum sources:** 5+

### **Performance Research:**
1. Find real user experiences (Reddit, GitHub)
2. Check multiple benchmarks
3. Look for different models/workflows
4. Verify hardware specs match
5. Check for updates/patches
6. **Minimum sources:** 5+

### **Comparison Research:**
1. Define criteria clearly
2. Find data for ALL options
3. Use same metrics across comparisons
4. Check multiple review sources
5. Look for real-world usage
6. **Minimum sources:** 7+

## 📝 Research Report Format

**Always include:**
1. **Methodology** - How did you research?
2. **Sources** - Where did information come from?
3. **Findings** - What did you discover?
4. **Confidence** - How sure are you?
5. **Caveats** - What's uncertain?

**Example:**
```
📊 Research Results: [Topic]

Methodology: Used Brave + Tavily, checked 7 sources (Reddit, GitHub, manufacturer, 3 retailers)

Sources:
- Reddit: r/comfyui (real user)
- GitHub: official discussion
- Manufacturer: official specs
- Retailers: Amazon, Newegg, Best Buy
- Benchmark: NotebookCheck

Findings: [What you found]
Confidence: High/Medium/Low
Caveats: [What's uncertain]
```
